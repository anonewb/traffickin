What is Human Trafficking?

The action or practice of illegally transporting people from one area or country to another, typically for the purposes of forced labour or commercial sexual exploitation.

How you can help to stop/prevent this crime?

Traffickin enables you to help combat Human trafficking by uploading photos of the victims anywhere even if the network is not avialable.

The purpose of Traffickin is to create a database of victims images so that an investigator can efficiently search, in order to find other images that were taken in the same location as an image that is part of an investigation.

Salient Features of this WebApp:

  • Can be added to your Home Screen.
  • Works Offline! i.e picture can be taken when there is no internet and is automatically posted to the server when the network is re-established.
  • Also other pages of the site can be accessed in offline mode as the pages are cached onces visited.
  • Uses your Geolocation to instantly detect your location while taking photo.
  • Notification is received when someone posts the picture.
  • Feels like android application.
  • Most of the above features works on normal desktop also.
